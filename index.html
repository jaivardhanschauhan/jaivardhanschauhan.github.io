Bio

Hi! I'm Jaivardhan. I recently completed an M.S. in Information Management (Data Science & Business Intelligence) at the University of Washington in Seattle. Before graduate school, I spent four and a half years in InMobi’s strategy-operations and programmatic-advertising units, supporting Fortune 500 clients like Mayo Clinic, Disney, and Zoetis across North America. At InMobi, I built machine learning models and analytics solutions that improved return on ad spend, cut manual checks, and helped the business scale campaign volume. For my undergrad, I hold a dual degree in Chemical Engineering and Chemistry from BITS Pilani. My toolkit is Python, SQL, R, Tableau, and the usual ML libraries. I like turning messy data into simple, useful answers that guide decisions. I’m looking for full-time roles in data science or analytics starting in 2025. Let’s talk.

As a Graduate Teaching Assistant for Generative AI Ethics, Programming, RDBMS, and Strategy coursework, I co-designed coursework and provided detailed feedback to 125 Master’s students across five classes. At the Paul Allen School’s Behavioral Data Science Lab, I worked on research on LLM-powered tools, reviewing papers, open-coding human and model rationales, and visualizing insights to help analysts produce higher-quality work. Back in India, I completed an Integrated Program in Business Analytics (IPBA) from IIM Indore. Throughout my studies, I also completed internships with Media IQ, L&T, and UW Advancement.
